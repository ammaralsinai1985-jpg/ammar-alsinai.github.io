About

I am a faculty member in the College of Engineering & Information Technology at Onaizah Colleges, Saudi Arabia. My work spans teaching, research, and academic leadership, including coordinating several undergraduate and postgraduate courses, supervising student research, and supporting the development of research centers and scientific councils.

I have published more than 60 Scopus / Web of Science indexed papers in international journals, collaborating with researchers across mathematics, engineering, and artificial intelligence.

Applied Mathematics Fractional Calculus Stochastic & Impulsive Systems Multi-Agent Systems Engineering Applications

Research Interests

  • Fractional differential equations, impulsive and stochastic systems, stability and oscillation.
  • Formation control and consensus of nonlinear multi-agent systems and vehicle platoons.
  • Mathematical modelling and simulation in engineering applications and nanofluid heat transfer.
  • Graph-theoretic indices, domination and Zagreb indices in complex networks.
  • Design of experiments, optimization, and reliability in engineering systems.
